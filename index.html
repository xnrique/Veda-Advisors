Veda is a contemporary art consultion firm. Working artists comprise Veda.

We are immersed in this world. We know the insides. We know how to navigate its corners from the most important angles, from the privacy of the studio and the public space of the exhibition arena. What works are on the rise? What works are overlooked? What art will stand the test of time? How do the right exhibitions happen? We build our practices and our relationships around these questions and this is exactly how we build our consultation.

Contemporary artists connect with other artists in ways that advisors do not. We build relationships with our clients around having direct access to artist’s voices, studios, insights, and dreams. In this same way, we build relationships with gallerists through their artists, rather than through sales alone.

We believe artistic acumen and first-hand knowledge of the processes of making and exhibiting art create the best collections, curations, and purchases.

Veda looks at the equation from all sides. We find out what works are hidden away and what projects are yet to be; we encourage commissions and pay attention to unrealized works. Unlike other firms focussed on answers, we are in the business of dialogue, questions, and visions.
